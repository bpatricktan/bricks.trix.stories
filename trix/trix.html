Hi! I'm Trix.

Born in the Philippines but raised in Kuwait, the country, not to be mistaken by Kawit in Cavite. Sorry, just had to clear that up since I've been thrown the same joke since I was 5! I'm a multicultural person, part chinese and part filipino, born in Bataan and spent the first year and a half of my life in Nueva Vizcaya. Before my 2nd birthday my parents brought me with them to live in Kuwait where they both worked. I was enrolled into an American school, but not just any American school, no no, it was an Christian American school! and thanks to that I've been fortunate enoughh to meet and befriend kids from all different parts of the World!

I got my first LEGO set when I was 5 and from then on I was amazed but what you could do with it. Every new set I got, I would first build it to the instructions then tear it down within the week to make something else entirely. My mother didn't really liked that, so she would glue some of my sets together! Never stopped me though. Lego has continued to challenge my imaginations, forcing me to think of so many different solutions to make the idead in my mind into reality, and look at my creations now. bigger, better, more complex. than the last.

It was actually my time playing with Lego as a child that nurtured my love of story telling. Creating back stories for all my little mini figures as they traversed a world made of brick. A little world made by me.
