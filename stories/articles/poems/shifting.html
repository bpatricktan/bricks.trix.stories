Shifting

I have never thought

that your smilecan melt my heart.


I have never thought

that your touch can break my walls.


I have never thought

that out of all people, you could pierce through me, whenever you wish.


I have never thought

That you can make me warm, warmer with your presence,

warmer if I could be in your arms, if you embrace my soul,

even my inner ghoul.


Your body is a wonderland,

I think of exploring it.


No, not in a physical sense,

but I'd like to stir up your emotions

and leave you how Yolanda left Tacloban.


But I think I ended up, being the victim.


Realizing all that, I have never thought that I'd fall into your abyss.


I was wondering, Can you guide me through?

Coz I really don't know where to go.
